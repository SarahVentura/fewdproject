Birthday Party Venues

Planning a birthday party can be hard, and expensive! Below, I have compiled information on different party venues in the Louisville area in order to make your life a little easier.

My Gym
  • Party packages starting at $240 for up to 20 children.
  • Privacy!- Party reservations are for the whole venue so you won't have to share the space with other people.
  • Paper goods (plates and napkins) and plasticware are provided by the facility.
  • Plus so much more! Visit the website for more information.

Skyzone
  • Party Packages start at $15 per child.
  • All parties receive one hour of jump time and 40 minutes in a private party room along with pizza and unlimited soft drinks.
  • Paper goods (plates and napkins) and plasticware are provided by the facility.
  • Plus so much more! Visit the website for more information.
