My Mission

Over the past two years, as I have raised my daughter, I have repeatedly wished for a comprehensive place to go for ideas on fun things to do with her. This really came to a head when I was planning her second birthday in November and was having to scour the web for information about where I could have the party at. I now give the fruit of my labors to you all to use as you wish. May the odds be ever in your favor. Enjoy!
